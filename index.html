Overview of El Salvador

Overview History Food Tourism

El Salvador is the smallest country in Central America, yet it is rich in culture, history, and natural beauty. Known for its volcanic landscapes and beautiful Pacific coastline, it offers a unique blend of indigenous and Spanish heritage. San Salvador, the capital city, is the center of government, commerce, and culture. Below are some key facts that provide a snapshot of the country’s geography, population, and national identity.

General Information
Official Name República de El Salvador
Capital San Salvador
National Language Spanish
Geography and Economy
Area 21,041 sq km/ 8,124 sq mi
Population 6,029,976
Currency United States Dollar/ Bitcoin
